About GitLogger

GitLogger is a project by the GitLogging Organization, dedicated to helping teams understand their codebase through meaningful metrics.

The Project

GitLogger provides metrics and insights into your Git repositories, helping you understand commit patterns, contributor activity, and the health of your development process.

Whether you're managing a single repository or coordinating across multiple projects, GitLogger gives you the visibility you need to make informed decisions about your codebase and your team.
